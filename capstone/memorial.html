1973-2023

David Breaux was a longstanding leader in the Davis community. He promoted radical compassion: that all people were deserving of compassion, love, understanding, and forgiveness. David, a self described "Street Therapist," who generously extended a compassionate ear to countless people within Davis.

David published a book titled "Compassion: Davis CA," shared hundreds of posts accross his social media pages. He was a son, brother, friend, author, community member and leader. His life was taken far too soon, and the absence of his physical presence is felt deeply within the Davis community. David's work lives on through the Compassion Bench, his online content, and through the intention of this website.

*All images of David included on this website were sourced from David's original online posts.
