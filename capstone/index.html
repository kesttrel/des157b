Welcome to the virtual Davis Compassion Bench

This space exists to extend the work of Davis community member David Breaux, also known as ‘The Compassion Guy’. David spent time on the Compassion Bench, located in Downtown Davis. On this bench he listened to other members of the community and discussed the meaning and significance of compassion in our lives. To learn more about David’s word please visit David's Memorial page.

Please take a few moments to reflect on your life and the concept of compassion. What does compassion mean to you? Where do we need more compassion? What does it mean to be compassionate?

When you have finished, please share your thoughts by creating a post.
